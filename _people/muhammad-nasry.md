Nasry is an Environmental Science graduate from NTU. When he's not ankle-deep in mud counting veins on a seagrass leaf or sighing at his seemingly endless inbox, he can be found in pantries battling his Coke (the drink) addiction (and failing). His work explores the connections between biodiversity, policy and society, with a particular passion for making biodiversity accessible and just a little bit fun.
