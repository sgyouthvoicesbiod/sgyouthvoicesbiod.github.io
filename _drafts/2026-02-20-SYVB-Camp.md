This weekend, we ran our first ever edition of our <strong> Biodiversity Bootcamp </strong>.
About 30 participants showed up for both days, and we had fun doing a wide range of activities!
We had an EIA workshop paired with a guided walk for participants to experience the wilderness and have a grounded view of what a habitat map would show!
